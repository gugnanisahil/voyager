ICONIC PLACES TO VISIT IN CANADA


Gros Morne National Park: Explore The Geological Diversity




This national park is one of the Canada points of interest you must definitely visit and explore. Geologically diverse and beautiful, it is a part of Long Range Mountains, which makes it even more attractive for a nature admirer like you! Enjoy nature walks and stroll into the wild at this stunning national park. ...Gros Morne National Park is the perfect backdrop for all kinds of outdoor activities, particularly hiking. Discover both marked and unmarked trails winding throughout the landscape, and explore the park's dense forests for rare plant, animal, and bird species. Hiking the Tablelands is an experience that cannot be forgotten. Here, where the theory of plate tectonics was confirmed, you can walk over ancient sea floor and preserved ocean avalanches. It’s a distinctive red landscape of exposed earth’s mantle, thrust up by the collision of tectonic plates millions of years ago.




Best Time: Late-May to ,mid-October
